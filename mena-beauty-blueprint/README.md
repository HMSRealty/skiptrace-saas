## Quick-start checklist (first 30 days)

- [ ] **Week 1 — Foundation:** Lock brand name + buy `.com` + register socials (IG, TikTok, Snapchat). Open Shopify trial. Pick theme.
- [ ] **Week 2 — Catalog:** Source 5 collections (20 hero SKUs). Negotiate a UAE/KSA 3PL for liquids. Set up payment gateways (Tap/Tabby for Gulf, COD provider for Egypt).
- [ ] **Week 3 — Build:** Install the 5-app stack. Localize store to RTL Arabic. Write product pages (use Module 5 templates). Set up WhatsApp automation.
- [ ] **Week 4 — Launch:** Seed 10 micro-influencers (Module 6 script). Film 14-day organic content. Start ad testing at low budget (Module 4).

## Core strategic thesis

1. **Two markets, two playbooks.** Egypt = high volume, **COD-dominant**, value pricing, Meta/IG traffic. Gulf = high AOV, **card/Apple Pay + BNPL**, premium pricing, Snapchat/TikTok traffic. Never run them as one funnel.
2. **Liquids are the moat.** Most competitors avoid perfumes/serums because of customs. Solving fulfillment for liquids (local 3PL stocking) is your unfair advantage and your defensibility.
3. **Trust is the conversion lever.** Arab women buy beauty on trust signals — UGC, reviews with photos, clear return policy, COD, and fast WhatsApp replies — far more than on discounts.

> ⚠️ **Disclaimer:** Specific suppliers, apps, shipping lines, and regulations change. Verify current customs rules (SFDA in KSA, Egyptian NFSA, Dubai Municipality) and gateway availability before committing budget. Treat tables as planning models, not guarantees.
